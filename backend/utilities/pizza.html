Homemade Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings:
    • Pepperoni slices
    • Sliced mushrooms
    • Sliced bell peppers
    • Sliced onions
    • Sliced olives
    • Fresh basil leaves
    • Other favorite toppings
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (for brushing)
  • Cornmeal or flour (for dusting)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface into your desired shape (round, rectangular, etc.).
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza peel or baking sheet that has been dusted with cornmeal or flour to prevent sticking.
  4. Spoon pizza sauce onto the dough and spread it evenly, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, covering the entire pizza.
  6. Add your desired toppings, distributing them evenly over the cheese.
  7. Brush the exposed edges of the dough with olive oil to help them brown in the oven.
  8. Transfer the pizza to the preheated oven, either directly onto the pizza stone or onto a baking sheet.
  9. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  10. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  11. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade pizza!

Variations:

  • If you like a crispy crust, pre-bake the dough for a few minutes before adding toppings.
  • For a healthier option, use whole wheat or cauliflower crust.
  • Experiment with different cheeses such as cheddar, provolone, or goat cheese.
  • Add a drizzle of balsamic glaze or sprinkle of red pepper flakes for extra flavor.
