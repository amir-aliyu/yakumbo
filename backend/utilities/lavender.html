Lavender Tea Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon dried lavender buds
  • 2 cups water
  • Optional: honey or sugar to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a small saucepan or kettle.
  2. Place the dried lavender buds in a teapot or heatproof container.
  3. Pour the boiling water over the lavender buds.
  4. Cover the teapot or container and let the lavender steep for about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Strain the tea to remove the lavender buds. You can use a fine mesh strainer or a tea infuser.
  6. Pour the lavender tea into cups.
  7. If desired, sweeten the tea with honey or sugar to taste.
  8. Stir well and enjoy your soothing lavender tea!

Variations:

  • Add a slice of lemon for a citrusy twist.
  • Combine lavender with other herbs like chamomile or mint for different flavor profiles.
  • Experiment with the steeping time to adjust the strength of the lavender flavor.

Benefits of Lavender Tea:

Lavender tea is known for its calming properties and soothing aroma. It may help with relaxation, stress relief, and promoting better sleep.

It's also believed to have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which can benefit overall health and well-being.
