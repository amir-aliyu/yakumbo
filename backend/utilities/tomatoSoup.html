Tomato Soup Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (28 oz each) whole tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1/4 cup heavy cream or coconut milk for creaminess (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and saut√© until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Stir in the whole tomatoes (with their juices) and vegetable broth. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Once simmering, use a spoon to break up the tomatoes into smaller pieces.
  6. Reduce heat to low and let the soup simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Remove the pot from heat and stir in the chopped basil leaves. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Optional: For a creamier soup, stir in the heavy cream or coconut milk.
  9. Using an immersion blender, blend the soup until smooth. Alternatively, transfer the soup to a blender and blend in batches until smooth.
  10. Return the blended soup to the pot and reheat if necessary.
  11. Serve hot, garnished with additional basil leaves if desired.
